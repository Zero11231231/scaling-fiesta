Philosophy Mission Vision Goals Objectives

Philosophy

The TAYABAS WESTERN ACADEMY is a recognized educational institutions that enhaces minds, cultivates values and lives in wisdom through holistic education.

Vission

To be globally competent, confident, competitive, and proactive learning community in nation building.

Mision

The TAYABAS WESTERN ACADEMY, the School of Choice, provides quality education that promotes a culture of excellence and instills positive values for responsible and productive citizenship.

Goals

   The TAYABAS WESTERN ACADEMY works toward the realization of these goals o create an engaging environment for academic support which enables students to reach their full potentials, develop 21st century skills, values and behavior needed to perform as confident, independent, and active learners.

Objectives

As an educational institution, Tayabas Western Academy aims to accomplish following objectives:

  • nurture a culture of academic excellence
  • develop competent, capable, and independent learners
  • instill the values of love of God and country, respect for all, honesty, punctuality, and concern for others, and
  • participate actively in social, political, and spiritual community affairs.
